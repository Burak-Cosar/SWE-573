SocialHub Community Code of Conduct


Introduction

Welcome to SocialHub! Our goal is to provide a vibrant, engaging, and safe space for all members to share, learn, and connect. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for member behavior as well as the consequences for unacceptable behavior. We invite all members to help us realize a safe and positive community experience for everyone.

1. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all members of the SocialHub community, including users, moderators, and administrators across all community platforms (website, social media, events, etc.).

2. Expected Behavior

The following behaviors are expected and requested of all community members:

  • Respectful Communication: Use welcoming and inclusive language.
  • Consideration: Be considerate and respectful in your speech and actions.
  • Helpfulness: Be supportive and look out for each other.
  • Collaboration: Collaboration and community cooperation are vital; share your knowledge to help others grow.
  • Constructive Feedback: Provide constructive feedback, focusing on helping to resolve issues and learning from mistakes.

3. Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered harassment and are unacceptable within our community:

  • Personal Attacks: Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, public or private harassment.
  • Discrimination: Harmful or prejudicial treatment or comments related to gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, mental illness, neuro(a)typicality, physical appearance, body size, race, age, regional discrimination, political or religious affiliation.
  • Inappropriate Content: Posting or displaying sexually explicit or violent material.
  • Unauthorized Sharing: Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission.
  • Advocacy of Harm: Promoting or encouraging others to engage in behavior that is explicitly harmful, such as violence, drug abuse, or hate crimes.

4. Consequences of Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior from any community member will not be tolerated. Anyone asked to stop unacceptable behavior is expected to comply immediately.

If a community member engages in unacceptable behavior, the SocialHub administrators may take any action they deem appropriate, including temporary or permanent banishment from the community without warning (and without refund in the case of a paid event).

5. Reporting Guidelines

If you are subject to or witness unacceptable behavior, or have any other concerns, please notify a community administrator as soon as possible. Additionally, community administrators are available to help community members engage with local law enforcement or to otherwise help those experiencing unacceptable behavior feel safe.

6. Addressing Grievances

If you feel you have been falsely or unfairly accused of violating this Code of Conduct, you should notify the SocialHub team with a concise description of your grievance. Your grievance will be handled in accordance with our existing governing policies.

7. Scope

We expect all community participants (contributors, paid or otherwise; sponsors; and other guests) to abide by this Code of Conduct in all community venues—online and in-person—as well as in all one-on-one communications pertaining to community business.

8. Contact Information

For any issues or to report a violation of this Code of Conduct, please contact our team directly at support@socialhub.com.

Conclusion

Your participation in the SocialHub community is contingent upon following these guidelines as well as fostering a welcoming and friendly environment. By ensuring respect and fairness for all, SocialHub remains a cornerstone for professional and personal development. Thank you for helping us make this community a great place for everyone.
